My Favorite Superheroes: A Semantic Webpage

DC Heroes

DC superhero comics are the best.
The storylines are more complex, and often address real-life social issues. My favorite is Green Arrow.

Marvel Heroes or whatever

Marvel superhero comics feel more 2-dimensional. However, Marvel is really killing it in the film and TV industries, whereas DC attempts to capitalize on the film and TV industries are more mediocre.

Conclusion

To summarize, Marvel heroes are not as cool as DC heroes.

But they do have decent movies and TV shows for whatever that's worth.
